Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply message any of our artists on WhatsApp with your requirements, and they'll guide you through the process.

Can I commission custom artwork?

Yes! We specialize in custom paintings. Share your vision with us, and we'll bring it to life.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and other local payment methods. Details will be shared after order confirmation.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we ship across Pakistan. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How long does a custom piece take?

Most custom artworks take 10–21 days depending on size and detail. We’ll confirm an estimated timeline before starting.

Can I request progress updates?

Absolutely! We share progress photos at key stages and welcome your feedback to be sure the piece matches your vision.

Do you offer framing services?

Framing is available on request for an additional cost. Let us know your preferred style and we’ll arrange it before delivery.

How should I care for my painting?

Display the artwork away from direct sunlight and moisture. Dust gently with a dry cloth to keep colors vibrant.

What if I need the artwork urgently?

Share your deadline and we’ll let you know if a rush order is possible. Priority requests may include an additional fee.
